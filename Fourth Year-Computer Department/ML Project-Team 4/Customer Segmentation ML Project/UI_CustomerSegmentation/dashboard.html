Customer Behavioural Analysis

Deep dive into customer personas generated via DBSCAN Clustering.

Cluster 0: "Active Low-Value"

Characteristics: Frequency very high, Monetary very low, Recency low, ReturnRate 12.5%, CancellationRate 32.5%
Insight: Highly active but low value.

Action: Monitor purchasing patterns, reduce returns/cancellations, educate customers

Cluster 1: "One-Time Buyers"

Characteristics: High Frequency, very low Monetary, Tenure = 1, CancellationRate 50%
Insight: One-time buyers / trial customers

Action: Target with retention campaigns, incentives to increase value and loyalty

Cluster 2: " Core Stable Customers"

Characteristics: Medium Frequency (107), medium Monetary (2.54), ReturnRate 0%, CancellationRate 0%, Avg_Days_Between_Purchases = 45
Insight: Stable core customers.

Action: Maintain engagement, loyalty programs, cross-sell opportunities

Cluster 3: "High-Value VIPs"

Characteristics: Low Frequency (44) but high Monetary (8.54), Tenure long (240 days), High Unique Products (106), low Return & Cancellation
Insight: High-value loyal / VIP customers

Action: VIP offers, personalized marketing, reward loyalty, upsell/cross-sell
